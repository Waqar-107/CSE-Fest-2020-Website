Events In The Fest

BUET CSE Fest 2020 is all about celebrations, competitive games and sports, intellectual discussions and mind-boggling contests! Programming enthusiast? Join our Hackathon event and become a participant! Exuberant coder? Show up at our IUPC! Enjoy your weekend in BUET's exhilarating campus during our Game Fest.

Don't forget to register for Artificial Intelligence Contest and Inter University Unknown Programming Language Competition. For BUET students, indoor games, treasure hunt and Quiz competitions await. The fiesta invites people to lose themselves in the soothing atmosphere of acoustic night and the thrill of of our cultural evening.
